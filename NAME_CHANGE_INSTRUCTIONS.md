# Name Change Instructions

## Georgia LLC
- File a name-change amendment with the Georgia Secretary of State.
- Use the online business services portal when possible.
- Save the stamped amendment for your bank, tax, and platform updates.

## IRS
- Keep the same EIN if you are only changing the business name and not the entity structure.
- Follow the IRS business name change guidance for your entity type.

## Field Nation
- Update tax/contact information inside your account where applicable.
- For the actual company/profile name, contact Field Nation Support and request a business name update.

## Also update these places
- Bank account and cards
- Insurance certificates
- SAM.gov, PIEE, FedConnect, and any contracting portals
- Vendor profiles and marketplaces
- Domain email signatures and invoice templates
- Google Business Profile and website footer metadata
